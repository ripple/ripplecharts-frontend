XRP Trade Volume

This visualization shows the trade volume of XRP both on the Ripple Consensus Ledger and off ledger, via markets on third-party exchanges. The data represents all volume during the rolling hour, day, 3 day 7 day, or 30 day window. This value can be represented in different currencies by selecting from the dropdown. The top number shows the cumulative value of XRP traded, while each exchange is represented individually below. The relative size of the circle represents the percentage of total volume that occured on each exchange, and each segment of the circle represents the volume of one particular currency pair.
