Multi Market Charts

Each chart is a 24 hour snapshot of the selected currency pair. the chart color indicates whether the price has moved up, down, or remained unchanged over the last 24 hours. The middle line and number indicates the current price. Click on any chart to see the live market data for that currency pair. These charts update every 60 seconds with the latest data from the Ripple Network.

Add new charts by clicking the "+" button, or remove them by clicking the "x" button at the top right of each chart. You can change currencies by selecting from the dropdowns at the bottom of the chart, and reverse the order by clicking the "Flip" button.
